A Legacy of Excellence

Our Story
Founded on principle

In 1987, William Harrington founded this firm with a single conviction: that exceptional legal counsel should be available to those facing the highest-stakes decisions of their lives.

Today, Harrington & Associates employs over 60 attorneys across six practice areas, serving clients from sovereign governments to private individuals navigating the most complex matters in modern commerce.

Our culture is one of uncompromising standards, intellectual rigour, and genuine devotion to those we represent.
